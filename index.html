Lemon Garlic Roasted Chicken

Welcome to Cooks Delight! Today, we embark on a journey of flavors with our Citrus Infusion Delight: Lemon Garlic Roasted Chicken.

1 HOUR Hard Prep 4 Serves

Masterpiece of Culinary Craftsmanship

Picture succulent chicken infused with the bright notes of lemon and the aromatic richness of garlic...

Do’s and Don’ts

  • Clean Hands: Ensure surfaces are clean to prevent cross-contamination.
  • Separate Boards: Dedicate boards for raw chicken.

Instructions

  1. Preheat and Prepare: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Rinse the chicken and pat it dry.
  2. Citrus Infusion: Lift the skin and rub minced garlic directly onto the meat. Place lemon slices under the skin.
  3. Herb Blend: Mix olive oil, thyme, rosemary, salt, and pepper. Brush the entire chicken.
  4. Roast: Roast for approximately 1 hour or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (about 3-4 pounds)
  • 2 lemons, sliced
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Nutritional Value

Calories: ~250

Protein: ~30g
