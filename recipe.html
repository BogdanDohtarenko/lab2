Decadent Chocolate Mousse

What you need:

  • 200g Dark chocolate
  • 1 cup Heavy cream
  • 3 Eggs
  • 50g Sugar

Step by step:

Melt chocolate. Whip cream. Fold together and chill for 2 hours.
